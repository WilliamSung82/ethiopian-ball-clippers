What level of animation intensity?

You mentioned animations and beautiful scrolling. How far should we go?

A

Subtle & Elegant

Fade-ins on scroll, smooth page transitions, hover effects on cards. Tasteful motion that doesn't distract. Think Apple product pages.

B

Rich & Immersive

Parallax scrolling, scroll-triggered animations, staggered card reveals, animated counters, smooth section transitions. Coffee beans floating in the hero. Eye-catching but professional.

C

Full Showstopper

3D elements, particle effects, cursor-following animations, scroll-jacking sections, cinematic page loads, WebGL coffee cup renders. Maximum "wow factor" — portfolio-grade showcase.

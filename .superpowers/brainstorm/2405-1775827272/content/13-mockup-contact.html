Frequently Asked

What's the minimum order quantity?

+

Our minimum order is 5kg per coffee. For first-time orders, we recommend starting with our free sample program to find your perfect match before committing.

Do you ship internationally?

+

Yes! We ship to most countries. Domestic Turkey delivery takes 1–5 days. International shipments typically arrive within 7–10 business days. Samples ship free within Turkey; international sample shipping is at cost.

Can you grind for Turkish coffee?

+

Absolutely — it's what we specialize in. We offer Turkish-grade extra-fine grinding on all orders. We can also ship whole bean if you prefer to grind in-house for maximum freshness.

How fresh is the coffee?

+

Every order is roasted within 48 hours of shipping. We don't hold pre-roasted inventory. Green beans are stored in climate-controlled conditions and roasted to order for peak freshness.

Are your coffees certified organic / fair trade?

+

Many of our partner cooperatives hold organic and fair trade certifications. We indicate certifications on each coffee's detail page. All our sourcing follows direct trade principles with above-market pricing.

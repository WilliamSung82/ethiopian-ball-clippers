Yirgacheffe Kochere

Gedeo Zone, SNNPR — 1,900m elevation

A stunning naturally processed coffee from the Kochere washing station in Yirgacheffe. Jasmine and bergamot aromatics unfold into a honey-sweet body with a clean, lingering finish. Exceptional complexity that shines in Turkish brewing — the fine grind and slow extraction reveal layers of floral and fruit character.

Turkish Brewing Guide

Per 65ml water. Extra-fine grind, powdery consistency.

65ml
Cold, filtered water. Fill cezve to just below the neck.

⏱️
Time
3–4 min
Patience is key. Let the foam develop slowly and evenly.

The Source

🌿
Kochere Cooperative Union
📍 Kochere, Gedeo Zone, Ethiopia

A union of 34 smallholder cooperatives in the Kochere district, representing over 4,200 farming families. They specialize in naturally processed heirloom varietals grown under shade canopy at high altitude. Fair trade certified since 2008.

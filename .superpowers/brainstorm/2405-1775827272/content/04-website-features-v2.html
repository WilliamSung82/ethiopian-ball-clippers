What should the website do?

You changed your mind — no problem! Select all the features you want. Click as many as you'd like.

1

Coffee catalog with filtering

Browse by country, region, roast level, flavor profile, price. Each coffee gets a detailed page with origin story, tasting notes, supplier info.

2

Inquiry / quote request form

Customers can request quotes for specific coffees and quantities. No direct checkout — you handle pricing and orders personally.

3

Direct e-commerce / cart

Full shopping cart with fixed pricing, online payment, and order tracking. Customers buy directly from the site.

4

Supplier profiles

Showcase the farms and cooperatives you source from. Maps, photos, stories — builds trust and transparency.

5

Blog / education section

Articles about African coffee origins, Turkish brewing methods, coffee culture. SEO-friendly content marketing.

6

Sample request

Let coffee shop owners request small samples before committing to a full order. Low-friction way to start a relationship.

The Journey

Coffee was born in Ethiopia. For centuries, the highlands of East Africa have produced beans of extraordinary complexity — floral, fruity, layered with nuance. Yet most of this coffee disappears into commodity blends, its character lost.

Meanwhile, Turkish coffee — the world's most intimate brewing method — demands beans of exceptional quality. The ultra-fine grind and slow cezve extraction reveal every subtlety, every flaw.

Ethiopian Ball Clippers exists at this intersection. We source directly from cooperatives across Africa and deliver single-origin coffees specifically selected for Turkish brewing excellence.

Our Journey

2024

The First Cezve

A chance encounter with Ethiopian Yirgacheffe brewed in a cezve changed everything. The floral notes that emerge in Turkish brewing were unlike anything we'd tasted.

2025

Direct Sourcing Begins

First trips to Ethiopia and Kenya. Built relationships with 12 cooperatives. Began cupping sessions with Turkish coffee shop owners to identify ideal profiles.

2026

Ethiopian Ball Clippers Launches

Full catalog of 40+ African coffees, each scored and profiled specifically for Turkish brewing. Direct partnerships with 25+ farm cooperatives across 6 countries.

What We Stand For

🤝
Direct Trade
We buy directly from cooperatives and farmer unions, ensuring fair prices and transparent supply chains. No middlemen, no commodity markets.
🔬
Turkish-Optimized
Every coffee is cupped specifically for Turkish brewing. We score aroma, body, and finish through a cezve — not just standard SCA protocols.
🌱
Sustainability
Shade-grown, organic-compatible farming practices. We invest in soil health and water conservation programs at origin.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked

What is the minimum order quantity?

Our minimum wholesale order is 5kg per coffee. For sample requests, there is no minimum — we're happy to send complimentary tasting samples to qualified businesses.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship worldwide. Domestic orders within Turkey arrive in 2–3 business days, while international shipments typically take 5–10 business days depending on destination.

Can I get my coffee ground for Turkish brewing?

Absolutely. Turkish grind is our specialty. All coffees can be ordered as whole bean or ground to an ultra-fine Turkish specification using our precision Mahlkonig grinders.

How fresh is the coffee when it arrives?

We roast to order. Your coffee is roasted within 48 hours of your order and shipped immediately, so it arrives at peak freshness. Every bag is stamped with its roast date.

Do you have organic or fair trade certifications?

Many of our coffees carry organic and fair trade certifications. We prioritize direct trade relationships, which often exceed fair trade standards. Specific certifications are listed on each coffee's detail page.
